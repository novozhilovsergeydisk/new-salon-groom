Discover Our Story

Founded in 2020, Karina Co. was born from a passion for pets and a commitment to excellence in grooming. Our mission is to provide a safe and welcoming environment where every pet receives the best grooming experience possible. We believe in treating every pet with compassion, striving for excellence in our services, and building trust with our clients. Our dedicated team, led by Karina Smith, ensures that your furry friends are pampered and cared for, enhancing their natural beauty while prioritizing their comfort and well-being.
